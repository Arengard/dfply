`cummax`
`cummin`
`cumprod`


v0.1.5
dplyr set operations added thanks to bleearmstrong.

`df >> union(other)`
Rows that appear in either `df` or `other`.

`df >> intersect(other)`
Rows that appear in both `df` and `other`

`df >> set_diff(other)`
Rows that appear in `df` but not `other`.
